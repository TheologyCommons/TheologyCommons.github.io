# PSALM 70 
## DAVID\'S CONCERN (70:1-3): 
###  They have slandered him! 
###  God should scatter them! 
## DAVID\'S CONFIDENCE (70:4-5): 
###  He will be rescued by the Lord! 
###  He then will rejoice in the Lord!
